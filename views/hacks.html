NITS HACKS 4.0

ABOUT

MAY 5&6

Here comes one of the grandest module of Tecnoesis, back again to cater the technical hunger of the masses. NITS HACKS 4.0 is all about providing the enthusiasts with a taste of the emerging technical aspects of real life applications. The idea is to bring together as many celebrated minds in the field of tech on the same stage where they can compete and build together.
